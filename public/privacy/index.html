Privacy

How your home data stays yours. Lumen is designed around local control, clear permission prompts, and no advertising or tracking SDKs.

Last updated June 10, 2026 Lumen for iPhone Version 1.0

Overview

Lumen is a smart home companion built to keep your home context private. It helps organize rooms, devices, scenes, and suggestions while keeping the experience local first.

00

Data We Collect

Lumen does not collect, sell, or transmit personal home data to external servers operated by us. Home data, including rooms, devices, scenes, automations, and usage context, is handled locally on your device unless you choose to use platform sync features.

01

Permissions

Lumen may request permission to access your smart-home configuration, location for presence features, and notifications for automation updates. These permissions are used only to provide app features you enable.

02

Smart Home Data

Device and scene information is used so Lumen can show controls, explain suggestions, and preview actions before anything changes. Lumen does not send that data to advertising networks, analytics brokers, or external profiling services.

03

Sync

If you enable platform sync features, some app data may be synchronized through your personal account with the operating-system provider. That synced data is governed by your account settings and the provider's privacy practices.

04

Third-Party Services

Lumen does not include third-party advertising, tracking, or behavioral analytics SDKs.

05

Children's Privacy

Lumen does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13.
