Privacy

How your home data stays yours. Everything Lumen knows about your home stays on your devices.

Last updated May 31, 2026 Lumen for HomeKit Version 1.0

Lumen is a smart home control app built on Apple HomeKit. This policy explains how your data is handled — briefly, because there isn't much to say.

00

Data We Collect

Lumen does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers. Home data — including devices, scenes, automations, and usage — is handled locally on your device and through your personal iCloud account using Apple's HomeKit framework.

01

Permissions

Lumen requests access to HomeKit only so you can view and control your HomeKit-enabled accessories, rooms, scenes, and automations within the app. This access is handled through Apple's HomeKit framework and remains local to your Apple devices and iCloud account.

02

HomeKit

Lumen uses Apple's HomeKit framework to communicate with your smart home devices. HomeKit data is managed by Apple, encrypted end-to-end where supported, and associated with your personal Apple account. Lumen does not transmit your HomeKit data to external servers operated by us.

03

iCloud

If you use iCloud, your HomeKit configuration may sync across your Apple devices through your personal iCloud account. This data is subject to Apple's iCloud privacy practices.

04

Third-Party Services

Lumen does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Your home is not a product.

05

Children's Privacy

Lumen does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
